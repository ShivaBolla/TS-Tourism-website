A Journey Through Telangana's Glorious Past

History of Telangana

Telangana's history spans several powerful dynasties including the Satavahanas, Kakatiyas, Bahmani Sultanate, and Qutb Shahi rulers.

Satavahana Dynasty

The Satavahanas ruled from 230 BCE to 220 CE, known for trade, architecture, and Buddhist influence in the region.

Kakatiya Dynasty

Famous for Warangal Fort and Ramappa Temple, the Kakatiyas promoted Telugu culture and architectural innovation.

Bahmani & Qutb Shahi Rule

The Qutb Shahi dynasty built Hyderabad and iconic structures like the Charminar and Golconda Fort.

Nizams & Modern Era

Hyderabad was ruled by the Nizams until its integration into India in 1948. Telangana became a state in 2014 with Hyderabad as its capital.
