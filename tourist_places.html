Must-Visit Tourist Attractions in Telangana

Historic Monuments

  • Charminar – Iconic 16th-century mosque and monument in Hyderabad.
  • Golconda Fort – Majestic fortress with acoustic marvels and stunning architecture.
  • Qutb Shahi Tombs – Tombs of rulers surrounded by serene gardens.
  • Warangal Fort – Architectural wonder of the Kakatiya dynasty.

Religious Sites

  • Yadagirigutta – Sacred temple of Lord Narasimha.
  • Kulpakji Jain Temple – 2,000-year-old marvel with exquisite carvings.
  • Birla Mandir – Temple built with white marble overlooking Hussain Sagar.
  • Bhadrachalam Temple – Dedicated to Lord Rama on the banks of Godavari.

Natural Wonders

  • Kuntala Falls – Tallest waterfall in Telangana.
  • Pochera Falls – Scenic location surrounded by forest.
  • Gayatri Falls – Hidden gem amidst the rocks and woods.
  • Ramoji Film City – World’s largest integrated film studio complex.
