Introduction

The Telangana State Tourism Development Corporation is a government agency promoting tourism across Telangana, India. Mr. Pervaram Ramulu, a retired Director General of Police, was appointed as its first chairman.

Telangana boasts a rich tapestry of tourist attractions: from iconic historical monuments and majestic forts to lush forests, cascading waterfalls, and spiritually significant temples.

Hyderabad, the capital city, secured 2nd place in Traveler Magazine's "Best Places to See in the World" in 2015.
