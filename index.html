Telangana tourism

The Telangana State Tourism Development Corporation is a state government agency that promotes tourism in Telangana, a state in the Southern region of India. The retired Director General of Police Pervaram Ramulu is the appointed First chairman of Telangana State Tourism. Tourist attractions in Telangana include historical places, monuments, forts, waterfalls, forests, and temples. Hyderabad, the state capital, was placed 2nd in Traveler Magazine's best places to see in the world in 2015.
